# Air Fryer Probe — Video & Motion

Guidelines for video, Reels, and motion graphics. Synced to the dashboard **Brand** section when published.

**Status:** TBC — video production pitch in progress. Expand when scope is approved.

## Aspect ratios

| Channel | Ratio | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Instagram Reels / TikTok | 9:16 | Primary short-form |
| Instagram feed | 1:1 or 4:5 | Prefer 4:5 for feed posts with video |
| Facebook feed | 1:1 | Match existing social templates |
| YouTube / website hero | 16:9 | Long-form and embeds |

## Brand on screen

- **Logo:** Use SVG variants from `company/brand/assets/logos/` — never retype "AIR FRYER"
- **Colours:** Dark Navy `#11151C` backgrounds; Brand Orange `#FD9E27` for headings and CTAs only
- **Typography:** Rubik headings, Roboto Mono for temperature readouts if shown on screen

## Filming & B-roll vocabulary

- **Subjects:** Probe on air fryer (hook), temperature check in basket, magnetic guide on fridge, UK home kitchen context
- **Avoid:** Wrong probe form factor, restaurant kitchens, AI-rendered logos, follower-count overlays
- **Colour grade:** Warm, moody kitchen lighting — align with lifestyle photography in `assets/images/`

## Audio & voice

- Match tone in `voice.md` — confident, direct, no *revolutionary* or *game-changing*
- Temperature claims must match `temperature-guide.md` (chicken **74°C**)

## Export settings (reference)

| Platform | Resolution | Frame rate |
|----------|------------|------------|
| Reels / TikTok | 1080×1920 | 30fps |
| YouTube | 1920×1080 | 30fps |
