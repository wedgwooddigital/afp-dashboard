## What they do

Air Fryer Probe sells a **3-in-1 precision cooking bundle** designed for the shift to air fryer cooking:

1. **Digital meat thermometer probe** — fast, accurate internal temperature readings
2. **Magnetic temperature guide** — wipe-clean PVC chart with ideal internal temps for all meats ("Goldilocks" chart)
3. **Self-adhesive hanging hook** — universal hook that attaches to the air fryer (or tile) so the probe is always within reach

The brand positions itself at the intersection of **professional kitchen technique** (cook to temperature, not time) and **everyday home cooking** (air fryer convenience).

## Origin story and credibility

Founder **Ben Cribb** brings **25 years in catering** to the brand — verified background in food trucks, cafés, event BBQ, corporate catering, and high-volume canteen operations across Bournemouth and Dorset. See [`trust-and-proof.md`](./trust-and-proof.md).

The founding insight: air fryers cook up to 30% faster than conventional ovens, making recipe times unreliable. Timing alone is not enough — internal temperature is the only reliable measure of doneness.

> "Pro's cook to temperature not time."

## Key differentiators

1. **Purpose-built for air fryers** — not a generic probe rebranded; includes hook designed for air fryer form factors
2. **Always within reach** — the adhesive hook solves the "where did I put the probe?" problem
3. **Temperature guide included** — removes the need to Google "what temperature is chicken done at?"
4. **Larger display** — 50% larger LCD than standard probes
5. **IP67 waterproof** — safe to clean after every use
6. **Fast reading** — 0.6 second response, ±0.5°C accuracy (0–100°C range)
7. **Versatile** — works with BBQ, grill, convection oven, and even baby milk formula
