## Physical description (for AI image prompts)

A handheld digital meat thermometer probe, approximately **160 × 36 × 18 mm**. Slim rectangular body in dark plastic with a **folding stainless steel probe needle** (304 stainless). Features a **prominent LCD screen** on the face (described as 50% larger than standard probes). The unit has a **caddy/hanging loop** at the top designed to sit on the included adhesive hook. Compact enough to hang on the side of a countertop air fryer.
