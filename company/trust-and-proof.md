### Approved phrasing (brand copy)

Use any of these — all verified:

- "25 years in catering"
- "25 years of professional catering experience"
- "After 25 years in professional kitchens…" *(acceptable shorthand — kitchens includes catering operations)*

Prefer **catering** when precision matters (About page, founder bio, credibility-led ads). Shorthand **professional kitchen experience** remains OK in product copy and social tips where brevity matters.

### What this supports in messaging

- "Pro's cook to temperature not time" — practitioner authority, not celebrity chef gatekeeping
- Cook-to-temperature education posts (safety, Doom Spiral, Liability Loop)
- "Chef-designed" is acceptable if framed as catering/kitchen practitioner — not Michelin or TV chef

## Do not cite in new copy

| Claim | Why |
|---|---|
| Facebook / Instagram follower counts | Low count adds no value; theme footer "300k Followers" is **wrong** |
| Klarna instalments at £23.99 | Klarna on site requires orders over £50 — not applicable at current price |
| "Cribbys" / legacy Amazon listing names | Legacy naming — use **Air Fryer Probe** |
| £19.99 introductory price | Historical promo only — current retail **£23.99** |

## Review sourcing (future)

When adding new social proof:

1. Prefer named UK customers with specific outcomes (dry → juicy, hook convenience)
2. Screenshot or link to published review where possible
3. Add row to testimonial table in `company/brand/voice.md` and this file
4. Never fabricate aggregate scores ("4.9 stars", "500+ reviews") without source
