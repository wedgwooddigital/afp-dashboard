## FAQ

Get answers to your most common questions about the Air Fryer Probe, easy, fast, and precise cooking every time.


### What is the Air Fryer Probe?

A precision tool designed to measure the exact internal temperature of your food, ensuring perfectly cooked meals every time.

### Is the probe safe to use with all air fryers?

Yes! It’s compatible with most air fryer models and built for safe, everyday use.

### How accurate is the temperature reading?

The probe measures from −50°C to 300°C with an accuracy of ±0.5°C between 0°C and 100°C.

### Does it come with any accessories?

Yes, it includes a **magnetic cooking temperature chart** and a **self-adhesive hook** for convenient storage.

### Is the probe waterproof?

Yes, it has an **IP67 waterproof rating**, making it safe to clean and durable for long-term use.

### What power source does it require?

It requires **2 × AAA 1.5V alkaline batteries**, which are **included** with the product.
