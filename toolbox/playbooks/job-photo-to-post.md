# Job photo → social post

A short workflow for turning a phone photo from a job into a publish-ready post without leaving brand guidelines.

## Before you start

- Photo shows the finished work clearly (or meaningful progress)
- No client identifiable details unless you have consent
- Check **Brand → Colours & logos** for logo overlay rules if needed

## Steps

### 1. Select and crop

- Crop to **4:5** for Instagram feed or **9:16** for Reels/stories
- Straighten horizon; avoid heavy filters — natural colour is on-brand

### 2. Write the caption

- Open **Toolbox → Social caption prompts**
- Paste the context block into your AI chat
- Use **Prompt 1 — Job completion post**
- Edit the output — remove anything you would not stand behind

### 3. Check voice and facts

- Read aloud once — does it sound like us?
- No invented offers, prices, or guarantees
- Phone number and CTA match current details

### 4. Publish

- Post natively in Meta Business Suite or platform app
- Reply to comments within 24 hours when possible

## Optional video version

For the same job, record 15–30 seconds of B-roll and follow **Brand → Video & motion** for aspect ratio and captions.

## When to ask us

- Negative comments or complaints
- Posts mentioning prices, guarantees, or awards not on the facts sheet
- Anything unsure from a consent or privacy perspective
