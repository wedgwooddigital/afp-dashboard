# Social caption prompts

Paste the **context block** once per chat, then use any **task prompt** below.

## Context block

```text
You are writing social media captions for CLIENT_TRADING_NAME, a business based in CLIENT_LOCATION serving CLIENT_SERVICE_AREA.

Voice: professional, warm, clear. Use "we" and "our". Short sentences. British English. No buzzwords. No invented prices or guarantees.

Services: [list from facts sheet]
USPs: [list from facts sheet]
```

---

## Prompt 1 — Job completion post

```text
Write 3 Instagram caption options for a post showing a recently completed job.

Include: what we did, one client benefit, soft CTA to get in touch. Max 150 words each. Add 5 relevant hashtags at the end (no spam tags).
```

## Prompt 2 — Seasonal tip

```text
Write a Facebook post offering a seasonal tip related to our services. Friendly and helpful, not salesy. 80–120 words. End with a question to encourage comments.
```

## Prompt 3 — Team / behind the scenes

```text
Write a LinkedIn caption for a behind-the-scenes photo of our team at work. Highlight professionalism and local roots. 100 words max.
```

## Prompt 4 — Review thank-you

```text
Write a short caption to accompany a screenshot of a 5-star Google review. Thank the customer without naming them unless placeholder [Customer first name] is used. 60 words max.
```

## Prompt 5 — Service explainer

```text
Write an Instagram carousel slide script (5 slides). Slide 1: hook question. Slides 2–4: one benefit each. Slide 5: CTA. Bullet points only, max 30 words per slide.
```
