# Google review reply templates

Personalise every reply — swap bracketed placeholders. Match tone in **Toolbox → Voice**.

## Positive review (short)

```text
Thank you, [Name] — we really appreciate you taking the time to leave this review. It was a pleasure working on [project/service] for you. If you need anything else, just get in touch.
— The CLIENT_TRADING_NAME team
```

## Positive review (detailed job)

```text
Thank you, [Name]. We're glad [specific detail they mentioned] met your expectations. Our team takes pride in [relevant USP]. We hope to help you again whenever you need [service type].
```

## Neutral / mixed feedback

```text
Thank you for your honest feedback, [Name]. We're sorry [specific issue] was not up to standard. We'd like to make this right — please contact us at [approved phone/email] so we can follow up directly.
```

## Rules

- Respond within 48 hours when possible
- Never argue in public — invite offline conversation for complaints
- Do not offer discounts or compensation in the reply unless pre-approved
